The National Fair Housing Alliance (NFHA) spearheads the fair housing movement, striving to end discrimination and promote equitable housing through education, advocacy, policy, enforcement, and community development programs.

Learning Resources
At MREEA (Maryland Real Estate Educators Association), we are passionate about empowering individuals like you to achieve success in the dynamic world of real estate. Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting your journey, this learning resources section is designed to provide you with a wealth of knowledge and tools to enhance your skills and expand your expertise.
Our mission is to foster a community of lifelong learners, where individuals can access comprehensive resources and stay up to date with the latest industry trends. Here, you will find a diverse range of educational materials, carefully curated to cater to the needs of aspiring real estate professionals, investors, brokers, and anyone seeking to gain a deeper understanding of the real estate market.
