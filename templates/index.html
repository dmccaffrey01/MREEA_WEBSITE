Introducing MREEA
Welcome to the Maryland Real Estate Educators Association (MREEA) - a dynamic group of real estate professionals dedicated to providing exceptional education and resources to industry practitioners in Maryland. Our diverse membership includes instructors, school owners, regulators, real estate attorneys, lenders, home inspectors, title officers, and anyone passionate about real estate education. Through quarterly meetings, we foster professional development, facilitate knowledge sharing, and engage with industry leaders to ensure we are at the forefront of real estate education in Maryland. Join us to enhance your skills, broaden your network, and make a lasting impact in the real estate education community.
