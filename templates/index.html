Who is MREEA?

MREEA unites real estate educators, including instructors, school owners, regulators, attorneys, lenders, inspectors, and more, fostering a comprehensive network
Join us quarterly to learn, connect, and engage with industry leaders. Elevate your skills and knowledge to excel as a Maryland real estate educator
MREEA empowers members through shared insights, networking, and continuous development. Together, we enhance real estate education in Maryland.

We know your struggles

Your often confused by the intricate nature of real estate education without any proper guidance or resources
It's challenging and time-contsuming to keep up with the constantly evolving regulations, teaching methods and industry trends
The feeling of isolation with no one to share experiences or connect with when a problem arises

We have your answers

MREEA simplifies the complexities of real estate education by offering valuable resources, shared insights, and a platform for collaborative problem-solving, making your journey easy
Through regular interactions with industry leaders, MREEA provides access to the latest updates, regulations, and effective teaching strategies
MREEA offers a vibrant community where real estate educators can connect, collaborate, and learn from diverse peers, eliminating isolation
